About

Let’s invest in a strong and timeless Greenpeace brand

This is a call-out for your commitment to futureproof the Greenpeace brand - so globally, we can reflect the relevance, integrity and values we hold as a proud leader of the environmental movement.

You mean this?

No, not our logo, our BRAND!

A brand is more than our logo. It is the visual, emotional and psychological associations that people make with our work. It's not what we say it is, it's what they say it is.

Why is strengthening our brand important?

Our victories have established Greenpeace as a globally recognised name. We are lucky that we have a huge, truly authentic brand. It makes our work much easier in many ways.

But Greenpeace is nearing its 50 year anniversary and with no real global brand guidelines, our identity is becoming more diffused and diluted.

Over the years, we have jumped from the success of brand attacks to believing that, to reach new audiences, we have to create new sub brands for every project. This has led to a vast range of project-specific graphic style-guides and a high level of visual inconsistency when viewed on a global scale.

As a result, in many locations where our work is most strategic, we are struggling with low or lowering brand recognition - often in the global south where the building of trust, relevance and resilience is key. These audiences, without clear brand cues, are less likely to connect our work with Greenpeace.

When our work is not associated with Greenpeace, we lessen the impact we have as a global organisation where credibility is our currency. Brands need to be recognised to be remembered.

But fear not. Help is on the way. There is a Big Plan to futureproof the Greenpeace brand.

What is the plan?

We can be unified without being uniform. We can be creative and speak in different contexts without losing our identity in the process.

As part of the big plan, we have identified the need for an accessible, consistent but agile global brand style guide. It is key to differentiating us and building a stronger and more resilient Greenpeace. Some offices, campaigns and projects have developed their own identities and guidelines, in part because an extensive global tool to guide us all is missing.

The Greenpeace Global Visual Style Guide will:

  • Provide a practical tool to help project teams and external suppliers create internal and external communication output inline with the Greenpeace brand.
  • Provide brand compliance guidelines to help manage brand consistency at a national and global level.
  • Increase the value and recognition of the Greenpeace brand by helping create visibility, consistency and repetition in all external and internal communication materials while giving enough variation to keep things fresh and relevant.

The project will be executed in three stages:

  • Stage 1 - ESSENTIAL visual style guide:
    (Vision, Mission, Values; Tone and Voice; Essential Visual brand identity - logo, primary colour scheme and typography; Photo and video guidelines; Essential brand compliance guidelines, Do’s and Don’ts)
  • Stage 2 - EXTENDED visual style guide:
    (Tagline and logo localisations; Secondary colour schemes and typography; Graphic elements and icons; Extended visual brand guidelines; Do’s and Don’ts;)
  • Stage 3 - Templates:
    (Internal communication materials: PPT presentations, other internal documents; External communication materials: digital, video, print, actions and other).

The brand style guide will be a living document that will be updated regularly to make sure that the tool remains useful and relevant.

Why we should all care?

The benefits of having a strong brand are many.

Here are just a few:

  • When fundraising: Positive brand recognition and differentiation builds integrity and is key to attracting new donors and ongoing brand exposure is key for long-term retention.
  • When campaigning: Greenpeace brand recognition and relevance has power to influence decision makers and move corporations.
  • When building people power and a billion acts of courage: Through making the Greenpeace brand more individually relevant we can help build the movement we need to do the work we must.
  • When things go wrong: A strongly supported and respected brand with a strong sense of values and integrity can help us through the attacks and backlash we face with the increasingly shrinking democratic space and nationalism.
  • For our supporters, volunteers and staff: Provides a strong platform that reflects our core values to proudly work from.

Why we need you: We are a team of passionate people from different NROs, holding different positions and skill sets. We will dedicate time and effort to make this project a reality but only YOU can futureproof the Greenpeace brand.

By financially backing this project, you will not only show your pride and support of the Greenpeace brand but also commit to use the style guide and follow the global guidance. The Global Engagement Department will match your contribution up to 35K and help the project!

The time is now, let's do this, together!

Let’s commit to and invest in a strong and timeless Greenpeace brand!
